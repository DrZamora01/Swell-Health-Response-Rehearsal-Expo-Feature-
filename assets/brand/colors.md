# Brand Colors

Theme colors used throughout the app, aligned with Swell Health branding:

- Background: `#0E1114`
- Surface: `#151A1F`
- Card: `#1C232B`
- Text: `#E6EDF3`
- Subtext: `#A8B3BE`
- Primary/Accent: `#0f766e` (teal-700) - Swell Health primary brand color
- Secondary: `#f59e0b` (amber-500) - Buttons, CTAs, highlights
- Success: `#7EE787`
- Danger: `#FF7B72`
